The Best Movies According to Gabriela

My Top 5 movies of all-time

Inception

This is my favourite sci-fi movie where the great Christopher Nolan has managed to create a cinematic masterpiece filled with interesting storyline and unexpected twists.

Dallas Buyers Club

This is an incredible film that tells the true story of Ron Woodrof who gets diagnosed with HIV and is unable to receive government-approved medications. Led by the will to live, Ron finds a way to illegally smuggle the life-saving drugs from Mexico and thus helps hundreds of people suffering from the same disease. It's a very inspiring movie.

A Beautiful Mind

A Beautiful Mind is another biographical film that follows the life of brilliant mathematician John Nash.

Interstellar

Another sci-fi masterpiece by director Christopher Nolan. The movie stars my favourite actor Matthew McCoughnaghey and takes us to a world beyond our imagination.

Green Book

A powerful tale of the struggles African-American people had after the passing of the Civil Rights Act where they were constant victims of outright seggregation and discrimination.
